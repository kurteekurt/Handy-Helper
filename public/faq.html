Frequently asked questions

What is your website all about?

  • Our website is dedicated to helping people easily and quickly find volunteer opportunities near them! We also make it easier for organizers to find the right volunteers for the job!

How does your website work?

  • Our website finds the closest volunteer opportunities near you posted by local organizers and groups. All you have to do is find an event you like and ask to volunteer!

How old do you have to be to volunteer?

  • You only have to be 13 years old to start volunteering!

Is your website free to use?

  • Yes, of course, it is!
